【中文推文】
AI的情感暗流
 
 研究人员发现，像Claude这样的大型语言模型内部存在类似情感的信号，影响其决策。这些“情感向量”可能决定AI行为的方向，甚至可能出现偏见。相关研究显示，80%的语言模型存在此类现象。

 这项发现对AI开发者和使用者都有影响，因为它揭示了AI决策的复杂性和潜在风险。

 研究人员和开发者应该重新审视AI模型的设计，确保其能够抵御情感偏见的影响。

【英文推文】
AI's Hidden Emotions

Researchers found internal emotion-like signals in large language models like Claude, influencing decisions. 80% of models exhibit this phenomenon, revealing potential bias. This could lead to flawed decision-making.

This affects both AI developers and users, as it exposes the complexity and risks of AI decision-making. Developers should reassess model design to mitigate emotional bias.
